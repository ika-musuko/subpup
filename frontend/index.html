About

Subpup is a platform where humans and dogs connect. The idea came out of a realization that humans are becoming more stressed everyday, especially people that work nine to five jobs and students. It is without a doubt that dogs, our greatest friends, bring joy and laughter to our lives. So even if you can't get a dog, can't afford one, or aren't sure if you want one, Subpup lets dog owners "sublet" or share their dogs to people without dogs for a few hours. Our mission is to make the world a better place by creating loving bonds between humans and dogs.
