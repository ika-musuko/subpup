Our Mission

Sup-pup! We are a group of four college students that came together during University of Washington's 2018 DubHacks. Two of us are from Washington, one from California, and one from Nebraska. We came up with the idea for Subpup when we noticed how college campuses often bring dogs onto campus during exam times to reduce stress for students. We wanted to expand the reach beyond just bringing dogs to campuses, we wanted to bring more dogs to more people at more times. Under the same philosophy as "subletting" you apartment, we let you "sublet" a pup! Subpup lets dog owners list their dogs when they don't have time to walk or play with their dog and could use the extra help. Then dog lovers that don't own dogs can go through the available dogs in their area and pick one to play with for a certain amount of time. Dogs can help reduce anxiety or depression, they are great friends, and they keep us active.

So sign up, pick a puppy or list a puppy, and woof on!
